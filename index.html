FC26 Recruitment & Squad Development Dashboard
Power BI DAX Data Visualisation Football Analytics

A two-page interactive Power BI dashboard built on top of the FC26 SQL dataset — taking the analysis from query to visual insight. Designed to simulate a real recruitment analyst workflow, from player discovery through to squad investment decisions.

  • Page 1 — Recruitment Analysis: KPI cards, scatter plot (current rating vs potential), donut chart (U21 vs senior split), ranked potential score table, and market value bar chart across 4,743 U21 players
  • Page 2 — 5-Year Squad Development Simulator: four budget tiers compared side by side (No Limit / 100–150M / 50–100M / Moneyball 0–50M) with multi-season rating growth line charts and per-player growth breakdowns
  • Key insight surfaced: spending 6x more on transfers gains only 3.4 overall points — smart scouting beats financial power
  • Full squad table with position, age, overall rating, potential, market value, and squad tier filterable by budget
